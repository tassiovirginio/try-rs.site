Why use try-rs?

Manage your temporary directories without losing focus.

Native Speed

Rust ensures try-rs is lightweight and responds instantly to every keystroke.

Fuzzy Finding

Locate your past experiments by typing just parts of the name. Fast and intuitive.

Clone & Try

Pass a Git URL and try-rs takes care of the cloning and organization for you.

Ratatui Interface

A complete TUI with windows, confirmation popups, and configurable colors.

Safe Cleanup

Remove what you no longer need with a shortcut, without risking important data.

Cross-platform

First-class support for Linux, macOS, and Windows. Consistent on any OS.
